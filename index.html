About

Trip Leader & Community Advocate

I'm interested in leading community events for youth and young adults that involve arts, design, and sustainability. In 2022, I volunteered for Canadian Wildlife Federation. In 2023, I led three 9-day canoe trips in Algonquin at a volunteer-run organization in Kingston, and I joined Outward Bound Training Academy for my first paddling trip down the French river! I'm eager to bring my skills and learnings back to my local community. Attached are my certifications.

Canadian Wildlife Federation 2022

I am a past participant of the CWF program. I was involved in hands-on conservation and environmental field learning. As a team we had an immersive 14-day paddling experience in Temagami. After our trip, we volunteered for 7 Minden sustainability organizations listed below. I am interested in collaborating with peers across the country to develop outreach and environmental service projects that can be delivered in 2023. I am looking forward to build a series of workshops to educate youth leaders, schools, and community members. Stay in tune for my community projects! Rock Painting , River Cleanup , Intro to Foraging , and Hiking .

Abbey Gardens

Created educational displays, maintained hiking trails, gardens, and volunteered at local festivals.

Walkabout Farms

Assisted disabled youth in therapeutic riding initiatives.

ULinks

Involved in bio monitoring of local fresh water lakes. Participated in water quality sampling, species identification, data collection and analysis.

Haliburton Forest

Built decks and signage using drills. Opened new trail for sightseeing.

Camp Outlook 2023

I continued my volunteer inititatives in Kingston as a trip leader in 2023. I was involved in route planning, food prep, and leading at-risk youth aged 13-17 on 9 day canoe trips in Algonquin Park. On the trip we played games, journaled, crafted with supplies found in nature, and practiced leave no trace principles. I'm happy to teach my skills, and pass on my passion of environment protection to those who tripped with me :)

Sprout Impact Fellowship 2023

With the generous mentorship and acceptance into Sprouts Impact Fellowship program, I started my own EcoCreatives initiatives! This is when I organize events in the Scarborough-Agincourt community to educate the public on conservation concepts throu nature walks and river clean ups, work with Toronto Public Library (North York branch) to host painting workshops to improve mental health, and collaborate with David Lewis Public School on pollenator garden to help the environment. I designed and built this webpage to showcase my volunteer work. Thanks to my friends, family, and volunteers for your help too!
